a·lo·ha /əˈlōˌhä/

a Hawaiian word used when greeting or parting from someone.

"aloha from the UK!"

(noun)

1. an utterance of “aloha”.

"from the first "aloha" everyone is friendly and attentive"

2. (in Hawaii) love and fellowship.

"what a great way to spread the aloha"
